Build a complete Android mobile application for a Product Purchase & Sales Management System with inventory, accounting, billing, and reporting features.

The app should be designed for small to medium businesses (like retail shops, distributors, agriculture product sellers).

### 🔹 Core Modules

1. Supplier Management

* Add/Edit/Delete suppliers
* Track supplier payments
* Outstanding dues (udhari)
* Supplier-wise ledger

2. Customer Management

* Add/Edit/Delete customers (buyers)
* Track credit (udhari) and payments
* Customer-wise ledger

3. Product Management

* Add products with:

  * Name
  * Category
  * Purchase price
  * Selling price
  * GST %
  * Rack/Location
* Stock quantity tracking

4. Purchase Management

* Create purchase entries from suppliers
* Update stock automatically
* Record purchase price, quantity, GST
* Generate purchase invoice

5. Sales Management

* Create sales entries for customers
* Payment modes:

  * Cash
  * UPI
  * Credit (Udhari)
* Auto stock deduction
* Sales invoice generation (GST & Non-GST)

6. Ledger System

* Unified ledger system for:

  * Suppliers
  * Customers
* Track:

  * Purchases
  * Sales
  * Payments
  * Balances
* Date-wise transaction history

7. Stock Management

* Real-time stock updates
* Low stock alerts
* Stock adjustment
* Item return handling:

  * Return to supplier (purchase return)
  * Customer returns (sales return)
* Handle both:

  * Item return
  * Money refund

8. Reports & Analytics

* Item-wise reports
* Supplier-wise reports
* Customer-wise reports
* Trend-wise (daily, monthly, yearly)
* Profit & Loss:

  * Item-wise
  * Rack-wise
  * Overall
* Purchase vs Sales comparison
* Export reports (PDF)

9. Billing / Invoice

* Generate invoices with:

  * GST and without GST option
  * Business details
  * Customer details
* Share invoice as PDF (WhatsApp supported)

10. Dashboard

* Summary cards:

  * Total sales
  * Total purchase
  * Profit
  * Pending payments
* Graphs for trends
